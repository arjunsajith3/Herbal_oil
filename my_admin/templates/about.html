Our Story

PureEssence Herbal Oils began as a small family project in the lush hills of Oregon. Our founder, Elena Green, grew up learning about the healing properties of plants from her grandmother, a skilled herbalist with generations of knowledge.

Frustrated by the synthetic ingredients and questionable sourcing in mainstream wellness products, Elena decided to create something differentâ€”a line of herbal oils that honored both nature's wisdom and modern purity standards.

What started as a small kitchen operation has grown into a company with global reach, but our core values remain unchanged. We still hand-select our herbs, use traditional extraction methods, and maintain personal relationships with our farming partners.

Today, we're proud to bring the healing power of plants to thousands of households while maintaining our commitment to sustainability, purity, and community.

Our Values

Purity & Potency

We use only 100% pure, undiluted herbal oils with no synthetic additives. Each batch is tested to ensure maximum therapeutic benefits.
